## Overview

To access your BenefitsInteractive dashboard:

1. Go to `https://app.benefitsinteractive.com`
2. Click "Sign In" in the top right
3. Enter your email and password
4. Click "Sign In" to continue

After logging in, you'll land on your personal dashboard where you can manage projects, view updates, and adjust settings.

<Callout kind="tip">
Bookmark the dashboard for easy future access.
</Callout>
