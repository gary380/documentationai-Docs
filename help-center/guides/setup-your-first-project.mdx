## Get Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in">
    Go to `https://app.benefitsinteractive.com` and log in to your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a project">
    Click "New Project" in the sidebar.
    Enter your project name and details.
    Click "Create".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite your team">
    Go to the Project Settings > Team.
    Enter team members' emails and send invites.
  </Step>
</Steps>
