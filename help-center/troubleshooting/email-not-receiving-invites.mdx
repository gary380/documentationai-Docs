---
title: Not Receiving Invite Emails
description: What to do if you don’t get your invitation email.
---

## Symptoms
- No invite email in your inbox
- Can’t join team or access BenefitsInteractive

## Possible Causes
- Email landed in spam/junk
- Wrong email address entered

## Quick Fix
<Steps>
  <Step title="Check spam/junk folder">
    Look for the invite in all mail folders.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check email address">
    Confirm your team used the correct email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Resend invite">
    Ask an admin to resend the invitation from the Team page.
  </Step>
</Steps>
