\# Getting Started (Employee)

**Role:** Employee / User

\## Welcome to BenefitsInteractive

BenefitsInteractive helps you understand your employee benefits. This guide covers the basics.

\## First Steps

1\. **Sign in** – Use your work email to sign in via Google or magic link.

2\. **Enter your Group ID** – If prompted, enter the 6-character Group ID from your HR department.

3\. **Complete your profile** – Add your name, phone, and department to activate your account.

\## Main Features

\- **Learning** – Watch videos and read guides to learn about your benefits.

\- **Binny** – Our AI assistant can answer benefits questions 24/7.

\- **Support** – Message your HR or benefits team directly from the portal.

\- **Compliance** – View and acknowledge required disclosures (e.g. ERISA) when prompted.

\## Need Help?

Use the **Help** button (floating icon) or contact your HR department.
