## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info" title="Before You Begin">
Prepare your materials including Summary Plan Descriptions (SPDs), plan summaries, carrier PDFs, FAQs, and any video content. Ensure you have admin access to your BenefitsInteractive account or sign up at `https://app.benefitsinteractive.app/signup`.
</Callout>

## Complete Setup in 4 Steps

Follow these steps to create your first interactive benefits hub.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Account" icon="user-plus">
    Visit `https://app.benefitsinteractive.app` and click **Sign Up**. Provide your email, company name, and role (HR, Broker, or Consultant).

    Verify your email and log in to the dashboard at `https://dashboard.benefitsinteractive.app`.

    <Callout kind="tip">
      Use a business email for team invites later.
    </Callout>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Upload Your Materials" icon="upload">
    In the dashboard, navigate to **Content > Upload Materials**.

    Use the web uploader for quick setup or API for automation.

    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="Web UI" icon="upload-cloud">
        Drag and drop your files:
        - `health-spd.pdf` (Summary Plan Description)
        - `dental-plan-summary.pdf`
        - `faq.md` or `faq.docx`
        - Videos: `open-enrollment.mp4`

        Click **Process Files** to begin extraction.
      </Tab>

      <Tab title="API" icon="code">
        <CodeGroup tabs="cURL,JavaScript">
          ```bash
          curl -X POST https://api.example.com/v1/upload \
            -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_API_KEY" \
            -F "file=@health-spd.pdf" \
            -F "type=spd"
          ```

          ```javascript
          const formData = new FormData();
          formData.append('file', fileInput.files[0]);
          formData.append('type', 'spd');

          fetch('https://api.example.com/v1/upload', {
            method: 'POST',
            headers: { 'Authorization': `Bearer ${YOUR_API_KEY}` },
            body: formData
          });
          ```
        </CodeGroup>
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Generate Your Hub" icon="zap">
    Once uploaded, go to **Hubs > Create New Hub**.

    Select your uploaded materials and choose a template:
    - **Open Enrollment** for seasonal campaigns
    - **General Benefits** for year-round access

    Click **Generate Hub**. Processing takes 2-5 minutes as AI extracts Q&A pairs and structures content.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Preview and Launch" icon="rocket">
    Review the generated hub in the preview pane. Edit Q&A, add custom topics, or tweak pathways.

    Test natural-language queries like "What is my dental coverage?".

    Publish with one click. Share the hub URL: `https://app.benefitsinteractive.app/your-company-hub`.

    Track analytics in **Insights > Usage**.
  </Step>
</Steps>
