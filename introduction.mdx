## Overview

BenefitsInteractive transforms your plan documents, FAQs, policies, and other materials into a single interactive benefits hub. Employees get instant answers to their questions using natural language, while HR teams and brokers save time on repetitive inquiries. Centralize benefits information to boost engagement and ensure compliance during open enrollment and beyond.

## How It Works

Follow these steps to set up your interactive benefits hub:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect Your Materials" icon="upload" title-type="p">
    Upload your SPDs, plan summaries, carrier PDFs, FAQs, and video content to your uniquely assigned Google Drive folder.
  </Step>

  <Step title="We Build Your Hub" icon="settings" title-type="p">
    Our platform converts materials into searchable Q\&A, structured topics, and customized learning pathways.

    ```bash
    # Example: Upload via dashboard or API
    curl -X POST https://api.benefitsinteractive.app/v1/documents \
      -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_API_KEY" \
      -F "file=@benefits.pdf"
    ```
  </Step>

  <Step title="Launch and Measure" icon="rocket" title-type="p">
    HR can track visits, top questions, and usage analytics to refine your communications and future benefits offerings.
  </Step>
</Steps>
