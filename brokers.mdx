\# Broker Guide

**Role:** Broker / Broker Assistant

\## Overview

As a Broker, you manage client groups, curate benefits content, and use analytics and plan comparisons to support renewals. This guide covers the main tools and workflows in the Broker Portal.

\---

\## Portfolio and Clients

The **Portfolio** tab shows all client groups assigned to you and lets you manage resources per group.

\### Key Features

\- **Client list** – View all groups assigned to you.

\- **Resource assignments** – Curate learning resources for each client.

\- **Drag and drop** – Prioritize content by dragging resources into the order you want.

\- **Customization** – Resources are inherited from the Master Library (if assigned by Master Admin) and can be customized per group.

\### Documents Vault

Share benefit guides and other documents with group admins via the **Documents** tab. The vault uses permission-based access so only authorized group admins can view documents for their group.

\---

\## Analytics

The **Analytics** tab gives you engagement metrics to demonstrate value at renewals:

\- **Engagement rates** – How often employees use the platform.

\- **Hours saved** – Estimated time saved on repetitive HR inquiries.

\- **Learning progress** – Completion of education modules.

\- **Compliance status** – ERISA and other acknowledgment rates by group.

Use these metrics in renewal discussions and client reviews.

\---

\## Plan Benchmarking

The **Plan Benchmarking** tool lets you upload plan designs, generate AI-powered comparisons, and share them with group admins.

\### Workflow

1\. **Upload plans** – Add 2–4 plan documents (PDF, DOCX, XLSX).

2\. **Generate comparison** – AI analyzes and produces a side-by-side comparison.

3\. **Save** – Save the report to your history for later use.

4\. **Share** – Share with a specific group so their Group Admin can view it.

5\. **Download / Print** – Export or print the comparison for meetings.

\### Visibility

You can mark comparisons as **Public** (visible to employees in the group) or **Private** (visible only to Group Admins).

**Tips**

\- Use clear plan names for easier identification.

\- Share comparisons before enrollment meetings for better decision support.

\- Reuse saved comparisons across similar clients when appropriate.

\---

\## Compliance

The **Compliance** tab shows ERISA and compliance status across your client groups. Use it to see which groups are behind, which employees need acknowledgments, and when to send reminders.

\---

\## Messages and Calendar

\- **Messages** – Communicate with group admins and clients from the portal.

\- **Calendar** – Manage and view events for your groups.

\---

\## CRM (Sales Pipeline)

The **CRM** tab helps you manage prospects and sales stages:

\- Track leads from New to Closed.

\- Log revenue projections.

\- Manage your pipeline by stage.

\---

\## History

The **History** tab stores your saved plan comparisons and other activities. Use it to reuse past comparisons or review what you’ve shared with clients.

\---

\## Need Help?

Use the **Help** button (floating icon) or contact support.
