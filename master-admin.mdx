\# Master Admin Guide

**Role:** Master Admin

\## Overview

As a Master Admin, you control the BenefitsInteractive platform. You manage groups, brokers, users, the Master Library, and system-wide settings. Use the Admin Dashboard at `/admin` to access all tabs and features.

\---

\## Registries

\### Groups

The **Groups** tab (Group Registry) lets you create and manage client organizations. Add groups, assign Group IDs, and control which brokers and users belong to each group.

\### Users

The **Users** tab (User Registry) shows all platform users. You can:

\- Elevate users to Group Admin or Master Group Admin.

\- Assign users to groups.

\- View and update profiles and roles.

\- Use **Impersonation** to view the platform as any user for support and auditing.

\### Brokers

The **Brokers** tab (Broker Registry) lets you add brokers, manage their profiles, and assign them to client groups. Brokers can only see and manage groups assigned to them.

\---

\## Master Library

The **Library** tab manages the Master Library—resources that can be inherited by all brokers. Content you create here can be assigned to brokers and, in turn, to groups. Use categories to structure content for easier mapping.

\---

\## Communications

The **Communications** tab supports system-wide broadcasts. Use it to send announcements or updates to groups, brokers, or users as needed.

\---

\## Compliance Hub

The **Compliance Hub** tab provides global ERISA and compliance tracking across all groups. You can:

\- Monitor compliance rates across the platform.

\- See which groups or employees need acknowledgments.

\- Track compliance events and status over time.

\---

\## Benchmarking

The **Benchmarking** tab (Master Plan Comparison Tool) lets you:

\- Run plan comparisons.

\- Assign results to brokers or groups.

\- Support brokers and groups with comparison data for renewals and enrollments.

\---

\## AI Documents & Chat History

\- **AI Documents** – Manage and ingest documents used by the Binny chatbot. Control which materials the AI can reference when answering benefits questions.

\- **Chat History** – Review AI chat history for support, quality assurance, and analytics.

\---

\## Marketing Leads

The **Marketing Leads** tab manages leads from marketing or prospective client forms. Use it to track and follow up on inquiries.

\---

\## Overview

The **Overview** tab shows system-wide KPIs: active sessions, engagement metrics, resource usage, and platform growth. Use it for high-level monitoring and reporting.

\---

\## Confirmations

The **Confirmations** tab provides confirmation reports for platform activities, such as enrollments or bulk operations.

\---

\## Impersonation

Use Impersonation (from the User Registry or user context) to view the platform as any user. This helps with:

\- Support and troubleshooting.

\- Auditing and compliance checks.

\- Understanding the experience of Group Admins, brokers, and employees.

\---

\## Need Help?

Use the **Help** button (floating icon) or contact technical support.
