\# Group Admin Guide

**Role:** Group Admin / Master Group Admin

\## Overview

As a Group Admin, you manage your team’s use of BenefitsInteractive and their adoption of benefits education. This guide covers team management, compliance, and common admin tasks.

\---

\## Team Management

The **Admin → Team** tab shows your workforce and lets you drive platform adoption.

\### Key Tasks

\- **Monitor profile completion** – See who has and hasn’t logged in or finished onboarding.

\- **Nudge employees** – Send reminders to team members who haven’t used the platform.

\- **Review activity** – Use the History tab to review engagement and learning progress.

\### Registry and Onboarding

Use the Team tab to:

\- Identify employees who haven’t activated their account.

\- Send nudge alerts to encourage full platform adoption.

\- Ensure everyone completes required profile fields.

**Tip:** Run nudges before open enrollment or key deadlines for better results.

\---

\## Benefits and Resources

The **Admin → Benefits** tab controls which learning resources and materials are available to your group. You can assign content curated by your broker and add group-specific resources.

\---

\## Compliance Hub

The **Admin → Compliance** tab tracks ERISA acknowledgments and other required disclosures for your group.

\### Features

\- **Compliance rate** – See what share of employees have acknowledged required documents.

\- **Pending list** – Identify employees who still need to view and acknowledge.

\- **Reminders** – Send batch reminders to employees with missing or expiring acknowledgments.

\- **Timeline** – See compliance events and status changes over time.

\### ERISA and Disclosures

When new disclosures (e.g. ERISA notices) are uploaded, employees receive notifications to view and acknowledge them. The Compliance Hub shows who has and hasn’t completed these steps.

**Best practices**

\- Review compliance before audits or renewal periods.

\- Use the reminder feature to follow up with employees who are behind.

\- Coordinate with your broker on any group-level compliance requirements.

\---

\## Benchmarking

If your broker shares plan comparisons, you can view them in the **Admin → Benchmarking** tab. Use these to compare plans and support renewal discussions.

\---

\## Need Help?

Use the **Help** button (floating icon) or contact your broker or support team.
